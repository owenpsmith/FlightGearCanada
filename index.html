Welcome to FlightGear Canada

FlightGear Canada allows Canadian FlightGear users and developers to share and discuss Canadian FlightGear subjects and development. Please visit our pages to share your development projects, download files, and add to the wishlist.

What's New

Wind turbines have been added to the scenery for all wind farms in PEI, see the map for loactions.

Mar 12, 2016 - BC wind farms added to scenery

Wind turbines have been added to the scenery for Bear Mountain (Dawson Creek), Eye of the Wind (Vancouver), Dokie Ridge (Chetwynd), Quality Wind (Tumbler Ridge), Cape Scott (Port Hardy), and Meikle Wind (Tumbler Ridge). See the map!

Owen.

Apr 21, 2015 - New content

Over the past few months I have added the following content:

  • an index of all known Canadian aircraft with 3D previews.
  • an index of all known Canadian liveries with 3D previews.
  • an interactive map showing areas of scenery development.
  • a screenshot page. If you have any Canadian themed screenshots, please submit them.
  • a links page
If you know of any liveries or aircraft I am missing, or want to add a wishlist item please let me know. If you would like me to add you to the developer or user profiles, drop me a line at owenpsmith@shaw.ca.

Owen.
